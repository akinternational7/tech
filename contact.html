Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Typically, a small to medium project takes 8-12 weeks from discovery to launch. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our 24/7 support team is always available to assist you.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Java, and cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.

How do you handle project communication?

We believe in transparent communication. You'll have a dedicated project manager and regular check-ins via your preferred channel (email, Slack, video calls).
